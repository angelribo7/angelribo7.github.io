Meet the Leadership!

Advaita Ayuluri (Founder and President) - Advaita is a student at Plano West Senior High School. Outside of school, she represents her school's debate team at various national tournaments, participates in campouts with her Boy Scouts troop, and enjoys spending time with her friends and family. Compelled by the inequities she observed in academic spaces, Advaita decided to start YVA to level the playing field for students of all backgrounds.
