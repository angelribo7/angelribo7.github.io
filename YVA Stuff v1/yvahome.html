Who Are We?

Young voices assembly is a 501c3 non-profit that is dedicated to breaking down barriers in educational spaces towards giving all a proper voice on the global stage. We help them find their voice and develop the skills they need to thrive in public speaking, communications, and outreach. We at YVA understand how important it is to foster healthy development from a young age, and expose youth to various educational opportunities like workshops, seminars, and hands-on learning experiences to help them set up their own platform for success.

We offer workshops, seminars, and hands-on learning experiences to equip kids with the tools they need to succeed. Because everyone deserves the chance to be heard.
